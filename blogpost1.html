Universities in Slovenia





Here are the main universities in Slovenia




Slovenian Universities:

— University of Ljubljana (Univerza v Ljubljana). It is included in the 500 best universities in the world by academic rating. The largest university in Slovenia. It has 23 institutes and three academies of arts.

— University of Maribor (Univerza v Mariboru). The second largest university in Slovenia. The School of Economics and Commerce was first established in 1959. The university received its status in 1975. At the moment, the university has 17 institutes.

— University of Nova Gorica (Univerza v Novi Gorici). It was formed on the basis of the School of Environmental Sciences in 1995. Renamed the University of Nova Gorica in 2006, it consists of four educational institutes, a school of viticulture and ecology, a school of arts, a graduate school and several well-known research institutes and laboratories throughout Slovenia.

— Primorsky University (Univerza na Primorskem). It is the third oldest and largest university in Slovenia.

— Nova Univerza (Nova Univerza). It has been working since 2008 and unites three faculties.

— University of Novo Mesto (Univerza v Novem mestu). It has existed since 2017 and unites four faculties.

There are also 35 private institutes operating in Slovenia. Basically, these are highly specialized institutions that pay special attention to the practical part of education. All private institutions in Slovenia have state accreditation. In the current programs, lectures and seminars are conducted by invited teachers from all over the world and professionals working in their specialty in successful European companies. Study tours to various companies throughout Europe are also organized.
