Accomodation in Slovenia





There are several options for student accommodation in Slovenia, depending on your needs and preferences.

Here are some common options:

University dormitories:

Most universities in Slovenia have dormitories on or near their campuses. These dormitories are usually the most affordable option for students, and they offer a great opportunity to meet other students. Average from 130 euros till 200 euros per month.

Private apartments:

Many students in Slovenia also choose to rent private apartments. This option provides more independence and privacy, but it can also be more expensive than dormitories. You can rent not just whole apprtments but just one room. Prices vary from 250 till 700 euros depend on location of flat and others factors.

Hostels:

There are also many hostels in Slovenia that cater to students and budget travelers. Hostels offer affordable accommodation with shared facilities.

When looking for accommodation, you can check with your university or educational institution for recommendations or search online for options. It's always a good idea to book your accommodation well in advance to ensure availability and avoid last-minute stress.
