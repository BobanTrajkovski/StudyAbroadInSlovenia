Language Courses





There are several language schools and institutions in Slovenia that offer Slovenian language courses for students. Here are a few options:

University language courses:

Most universities in Slovenia offer Slovenian language courses for international students. These courses are often designed specifically for beginners and can help students improve their language skills while studying in Slovenia.

Private language schools:

There are several private language schools in Slovenia that offer Slovenian language courses for students of all levels. These schools usually offer intensive courses that allow students to learn the language quickly.

Summer language programs:

Many language schools and institutions in Slovenia offer summer language programs for students. These programs are usually held during the summer months and provide an immersive language experience that includes language classes, cultural activities, and excursions.
